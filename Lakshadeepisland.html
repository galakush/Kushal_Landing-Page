Lakshadweep Islands

Embrace the serene beauty of Lakshadweep, India's smallest Union Territory known for its exotic and sun-kissed beaches and lush green landscape. Here are some of the top attractions to explore:

Explore the Beauty - Video Tour

Minicoy Island

Minicoy Island, the southernmost island of Lakshadweep, is renowned for its vibrant coral reefs, white sandy beaches, and the traditional culture of the local Maldivean community. It's a perfect spot for water sports enthusiasts.

Agatti Island

Agatti Island is known for its blissfully secluded beaches and the stunning turquoise-blue lagoon surrounding it. Agatti offers a mesmerizing experience with activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking.

Bangaram Atoll

Bangaram Atoll is a tiny teardrop-shaped island, offering solitude in the lap of nature. Surrounded by a shallow lagoon enclosed by coral reef, it provides an ideal setting for snorkeling, deep-sea fishing, and diving.

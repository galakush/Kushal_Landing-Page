Exploring Indian Islands

Welcome to our page dedicated to the beautiful islands of India. Here, we explore some of the most stunning and less-known islands that offer breathtaking views, unique cultures, and unforgettable experiences.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Located in the Bay of Bengal, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a group of over 300 islands, known for their pristine beaches, clear water, and vibrant coral reefs. Ideal for snorkeling, scuba diving, and exploring rich marine life.

Lakshadweep Islands

Off the coast of Kerala, the Lakshadweep Islands are a tropical archipelago of 36 atolls and coral reefs. These islands are famous for their stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and diverse aquatic life.
